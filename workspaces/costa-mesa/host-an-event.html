Host a Gathering at Wayfare

Wayfare Costa Mesa's clean, minimalistic design makes it perfectly suited for a wide variety of tastes and styles, where you are free to add your own personal touches and accomplish the exact feel you desire. The open layout, high industrial ceilings, vaulted skylights, and dazzling sophisticated chandeliers create the perfect canvas and spotlight for your unique occasion.

Wayfare is ideally suited for workshops, business events & non-profit gatherings; but we would love to learn about whatever your occassion may be! Choose your event category below for more information & to submit your inquiry.

Social Enterprise & Non-Profit Gatherings

We absolute love being able to partner with incredible non-profits and social enterprises to bring their mission to life in the form of fundraiser events, trainings, meetings, retreats, and more! We want you to be creative and use the space to best fit the mission and vision of your organization. Tell us a little bit more about your organization and your vision for this event and we'll get back to you in no time!
